## AI helps us solve non-trivial parameterization problems

Says you have a bunch of images and you want to classify them into cat or dog. How do you know which pixel is important?
How do you define a feature of cat and dog **exhaustively**? How do you order the importance of each feature?

You don't. You just exaggerate the parameters and chuck a tons of data into the model and let it figure out how to mimic the reality.
